# Study in Australia - Complete Guide

## Why Study in Australia

### Key Advantages
1. **Top 100 Universities**
   - 8 Australian Universities among world's top 100

2. **Stable Economy**
   - One of the world's most stable economies

3. **4 Years Work Visa**
   - Post-study work visa up to 4 years

4. **High Scholarships**
   - High probability for PG and Research programs

## Admission Process Steps

1. **Document Preparation** - Gather academic and personal documents
2. **University Application** - Submit application to chosen universities
3. **Offer Letter** - Receive conditional or unconditional offer of admission
4. **Scholarship Assessment** - Apply for and await scholarship results
5. **Health Insurance** - Obtain OSHC (Overseas Student Health Cover)
6. **Visa Application** - Apply for student visa (typically online via ImmiAccount)
7. **Visa Grant** - Receive visa approval
8. **Pre-Departure** - Finalize travel and accommodation arrangements
9. **University Enrollment** - Register and commence studies

## Key Information

### Study Visa Details
- **Duration**: Based on program length plus grace period
- **Visa Type**: Subclass 500 (Student Visa)
- **Work Authorization**: Up to 40 hours/fortnight during semester, unlimited during breaks
- **Post-Study Work Visa**: 1.5-4 years depending on field and qualification level

### Cost Breakdown
- **Tuition**: AUD $30,000 - $50,000/year (varies by university and field)
- **Living Expenses**: AUD $20,000 - $30,000/year
- **Health Insurance (OSHC)**: AUD $1,500 - $2,500/year
- **Total Average**: AUD $55,000 - $85,000/year

### Intake Periods
- **February/March (Semester 1)** - Main intake
- **July/August (Semester 2)** - Secondary intake

### English Language Requirements
- IELTS: 6.0 - 7.5 (depending on program)
- TOEFL: 80 - 100 (internet-based)
- PTE: 50 - 79
- Duolingo: 110-120

### Work Opportunities
- On-campus employment (libraries, catering, administration)
- Off-campus work (restaurants, retail, professional roles)
- Internships and co-op placements increasingly available
- Strong pathways to permanent residency upon graduation

---

## Important Notes
- Processing time: 4-8 weeks for visa application
- Tuition fees among the highest globally but offset by work visa duration
- Scholarship opportunities with relatively high success rates
- Strong job market with competitive salaries
- Excellent quality of life and safety
- Networking opportunities with Indian diaspora
