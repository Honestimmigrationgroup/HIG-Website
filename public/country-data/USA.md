# Study in USA - Complete Guide

## Why Study in USA

### Key Advantages
1. **Most Prestigious Universities**
   - Home to world's top-ranked universities in global rankings

2. **Highest Scholarships Available**
   - Extensive scholarship opportunities for international students

3. **Flexible Admission & Curriculum**
   - Diverse admission pathways and flexible curriculum options

4. **12-36 Months Work Visa**
   - Post-graduation work opportunities with OPT programs

## Key Information

### Study Visa Details
- **Work Opportunities**: OPT (Optional Practical Training) allows 12-36 months of work after graduation depending on field of study
- **Visa Type**: F-1 Student Visa

### Cost Considerations
- Varies by university and location
- Scholarships widely available for international students
- Living costs vary by state (California and New York typically higher)

### Duration
- Bachelor's: 4 years
- Master's: 2 years
- PhD: 5-7 years

---

## Important Notes
- Early application is recommended (6-9 months before intake)
- Multiple scholarship deadlines throughout the year
- Work authorization available during and after studies
- Strong networking opportunities through campus activities and alumni networks
