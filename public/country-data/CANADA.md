## Admission Process Steps

1. **Document Preparation** - Gather all required documents
2. **Application Process** - Submit applications directly to colleges
3. **Offer Letter** - Receive admission offer from institution
4. **Medical Examination** - Undergo prescribed medical tests
5. **GIC Account Opening** - Open Guaranteed Investment Certificate account
6. **Visa Application** - Apply for study permit
7. **Visa Grant** - Receive study permit approval
8. **Pre-Departure** - Arrange travel and accommodation
9. **College Enrollment** - Register and begin studies

## Key Information

### Study Visa Details
- **Duration**: Based on program length plus optional practical training
- **GIC Requirement**: Generally CAD $20,000 - $30,000 depending on program
- **Work Authorization**: On-campus and off-campus work available
- **Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)**: Available for 8 months to 3 years depending on program length

### Cost Breakdown
- **Tuition**: CAD $16,000 - $20,000/year (varies by college and program)
- **Living Expenses**: CAD $15,000 - $20,000/year
- **Total Average**: CAD $35,000 - $40,000/year

### Intake Periods
- **Fall (September)** - Most common intake
- **Winter (January)** - Less common, selected programs
- **Spring (May)** - Some programs available

### English Language Requirements
- IELTS: Generally 6.0 - 6.5 IELTS band score
- TOEFL: 75 - 90 (internet-based)
- Duolingo English Test: 105 - 120

---

## Important Notes
- Processing time: 4-6 months from application to admission
- Medical examination required before visa
- Proof of financial support mandatory
- Valid passport required (minimum 2 years validity)
- Travel insurance recommended
- Strong employment prospects post-graduation
