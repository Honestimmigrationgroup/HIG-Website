## General European Information

### Study Visa Details
- Varies by country (Schengen visa for most)
- Duration based on program length
- Post-graduation work permits available in most countries
- Health insurance requirements vary

### English Language Requirements (Typical)
- IELTS: 6.0 - 7.0
- TOEFL: 80 - 100
- Most universities accept English for Master's programs
- Bachelor's often requires local language proficiency

### General Cost Range

- Central Europe (Germany, Austria): â‚¬8,000 - â‚¬25,000/year

- Eastern Europe: â‚¬6,000 - â‚¬15,000/year

### Work Opportunities
- Varies by country and visa type
- Most countries allow 10-20 hours/week during studies
- Post-graduation work permits: 1-3 years depending on country
- High employment rates for graduates in most countries

### Intake Periods
- **Fall (September)** - Main intake across Europe
- **Spring (February/March)** - Limited programs
- Summer programs available at many institutions

### Travel & Mobility
- Schengen agreement allows travel across member countries
- ERASMUS+ program enables student exchange
- Low travel costs between countries
- Rich cultural experiences and networking

## Important Notes
- Research visa requirements carefully for your chosen country
- Language programs often available for non-English speakers
- Scholarships: DAAD (Germany), Erasmus Mundus, country-specific options
- Processing time: 2-4 months typically
- Application deadlines: Usually December-January for Fall intake
- Strong academic reputation globally
- Excellent pathway to EU residency in many countries
- Growing tech hubs (Berlin, Dublin, Amsterdam)
